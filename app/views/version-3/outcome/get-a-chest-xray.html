You need a chest X-ray in the next few days

Sometimes having a cough or other symptoms for more than 3 weeks can be a sign of lung cancer.

A chest X-ray will help doctors decide what other tests and investigations you might need.

The whole process should not take more than half an hour.

You do not need to contact your GP until the results of the X-ray are back.

Important: Important

It's important to remember that only 1 in 100 people are diagnosed with lung cancer following a chest X-ray.
