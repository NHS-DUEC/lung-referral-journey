What happens during the chest X-ray

When you arrive at the hospital you will usually wait no more than about 10 minutes. The X-ray takes about 20 minutes.

You will undress to the waist and be given a hospital gown to wear. You keep your belongings with you.

The results will be sent to your GP surgery about 2 weeks after the X-ray.
