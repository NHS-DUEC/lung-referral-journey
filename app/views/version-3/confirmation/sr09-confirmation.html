What happens during the chest X-ray

When you arrive at the hospital you will usually wait no more than about 10 minutes. The X-ray takes about 20 minutes.

You will undress to the waist and be given a hospital gown to wear. You keep your belongings with you.

Find out more about what happens during an X-ray

Contacting your GP surgery for the results

The results will be sent to your GP surgery about 2 weeks after the X-ray.

You must contact them to find out what happens next.

What to do if you feel worse

Use 111 online again or call 111 if:

  • your symptoms get worse
  • you have any new symptoms

If you are deaf or hard of hearing there are other ways you can contact 111.
