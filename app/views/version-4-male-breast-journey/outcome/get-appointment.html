Get an appointment at a breast clinic

Breast clinics see men and women.

You can refer yourself to a one-stop clinic without having to see your GP first.

They will check your symptoms to see what might be causing them.

How to get an appointment

We will ask you for some personal details and send them to your local breast clinic.

The breast clinic will then contact you to agree a date and time.

Get an appointment

Should I contact my GP surgery?

There is no need to contact your GP. You will save time by referring yourself to the clinic now.

But if you would rather speak to your GP instead, contact them within the next few days.

If your GP thinks your symptoms need to be checked they will refer you to the same clinic for the same tests.

Important

Breast cancer in men is very rare, but it's important to get any symptoms checked out.
